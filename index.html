About

Young pianist Teresa He was born in Beijing and started her journey with piano before the age of 4. She joined Dr. Wenli Zhou's studio in 2014 and exploring her dynamics in Greater Houston Area. Before Collage, she played recitals and chamber music in Beijing and Dalian. Teresa studied Bachelor of Music in piano performance at University of Texas at Austin in 2016 with Prof. David Renner and Prof. Andrew Bowen. She showed her great interest in chamber music as well, founded Pastel Piano Trio in freshman year and studied with Miro Quartet and pianist Patti Wolf.
